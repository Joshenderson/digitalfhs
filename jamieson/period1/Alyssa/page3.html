The Band

The Symphonic Band has a variety of students. Any type of student is welcome to join. It's best recommended to have at least 2 years of experience with your instrument. Because of the eliminated beginning band, students who want to learn an instrument will have a harder time. In Symphonic band, with its busy schedule, it is hard for students, who learn a new instrument, get the proper amount of one on one time with the teacher. Luckily, fellow students may help the new student in teaching the fingerings for the notes.

This will show you the history of Symphonic bands.
