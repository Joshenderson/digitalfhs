Our Music

Our music has a wide range of genres. From the classics, like Beethoven, to more modern pieces, like The Lion King.

We are prepared to sight read all types of music, and hopefully perfect them before June.

We dont't just play this music to entertain others, we do it to entertain ourselves.

Types of Music:

Beethoven and other classics

Classic cartoons

Jazz

Marching pieces

Modern movies

Student arrangements

And More!!!

The Classics

The classics range from the 1700s with Beethoven and Mozart, to the 1900s with The Pink Panther and Popeye.

Learning and performing these classics can give an unforgetable experience to our generation.

Most of these are recognized easily by the audience.

Classics We Play:

Little Fugue By: Johann Sebastian Bach

Washington Post March By: John Philip Sousa

The Olympic Spirit By: John Williams

Powerhouse By: Paul Murtha

And More!!!

Modern Music

The modern music we play usually are based off movies, such as The Lion King and Frozen. Believe it or not, modern music seems more difficult since majority of the band has watched these movies. Sometimes, half the band likes it,

and half the band dislike it. For example; students like The Lion King, but dislike playing it because its 6 pages long and is 15 minutes from start to finish. That's why most modern pieces, we wouldn't perform at concerts.

Modern Music We Play:

The Nightmare Before Christmas By: Michael Brown

The Pirates of the Caribbean By: Jay Bocook

Star Trek (new version) By: Jay Bocook

The Lion King By: Calvin Custer

Frozen By: Stephen Bulla

And More!!!
